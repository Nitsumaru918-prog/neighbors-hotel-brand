Area Guide

Local Spots

From nearby spots within walking distance to sightseeing destinations a short train ride away.

01
Shrine 2 min walk

Tenso Jinja

Walk 2 min

A historic Shinto shrine just steps from the hotel, founded in the 1320s during the late Kamakura period. Dedicated to Amaterasu-Omikami, it remains beloved in the neighborhood especially during the September mikoshi festival.

Official site Open in Google Maps →
02
University approx. 24 min

Waseda University

Walk 4 min to Otsuka-Ekimae
→
Toden Arakawa Line approx. 12 min Otsuka-Ekimae → Waseda
→
Walk 8 min from Waseda station

Ranked 2nd in Japan's "Truly Strong Universities 2026." Founded in 1882, this prestigious institution draws 40,000 students. Its expansive campus is also a popular visitor destination in its own right.

Official site Open in Google Maps →
03
Complex 13 min walk

Ikebukuro Sunshine City

Walk 13 min

A landmark urban complex centered on a skyscraper, packed with aquarium, observation deck, anime shops, shopping mall, museum, and cinema. An all-day entertainment destination for the whole family.

Open in Google Maps →
04
Shopping Street 2 min walk

Sunmall Otsukadai Shopping Street

Walk 2 min

A charming old-fashioned shopping street adjacent to Tenso Jinja, with a unique torii gate as its entrance. Traditional local shops line the streets of this shitamachi-style arcade stretching from Otsuka Station's south exit.
